Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Athlon collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you register and use Athlon, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and encrypted password.
  • Physical Data: Height, weight, activity level, and goals provided during onboarding to calculate your TDEE.
  • Activity Logs: Meals logged, workouts logged, and daily calorie balances.
  • AI Conversations: Messages and context shared with our AI agent, PT Coach Max, for the purpose of analyzing and assisting with your fitness goals.

2. How We Use Your Information

All data collected will be kept in the app owner's database and is used exclusively for providing and improving the Athlon service. This includes calculating your calorie bank, storing your historical records, and personalizing the coaching experience with Max.

3. Third-Party Services

We use external services (like Groq for our AI coach and the USDA database for food search). The information shared with these services is strictly limited to the context needed to process your requests (e.g., your chat queries and relevant logs required for the AI to make a decision). We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures, including JSON Web Tokens (JWT) for authentication and bcrypt for password hashing, to protect your personal information. However, no internet transmission is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. Continued use of the app constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
